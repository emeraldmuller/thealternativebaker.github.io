About The Alternative Baker

About Me

My name is Emerald. I am currently the only person running this site, but I hope to have the help of others in the future. I have dedicated my time learning how to create recipes that fit a variety of diets. My inspiration for this initially came from the fact that I have both food allergies and a gluten intolerance and I did not want to have to cut out my favorite foods from my diet. Along the way, I learned that others were in the same position and that adapting recipes for other alternative diets wasn't much different than what I was doing for my own diet.

My Philosophy

I do not view unconventional diets as inconveniences, rather, I see them as an opportunity to learn a new way of baking. I know what it is like to be judged for having a certain diet, so I do not want to judge others for having a diet that is different from my own. I like to challenge myself to learn to create recipes that fit these diets but are also enjoyable. I also hope to help people realize that no matter the diet, there can still be good quality food.
