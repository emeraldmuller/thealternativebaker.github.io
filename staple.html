Staple Ingredients

The ingredients listed here can be used in recipes across all diets. They can be used as substitutes for common ingredients in traditional recipes and will make frequent appearances on this site.

1. Flax Meal or Flaxseeds

Flax meal can be used as an egg substitute for the purpose of binding ingredients when added to water. Flaxseeds can be grounded up to make flax meal. Recommended brands: Spectrum Essentials Ground Flaxseed , Bob's Red Mill Ground Flaxseed Meal .

2. One to One Gluten Free Flour Blend

One to one flour blends are typically made up of rice flour, potato startch, tapioca flour, and sorghum flour. Most of them are free of nuts. These flour blends create a texture that mimics that of regular flour. For recipes that are striclty gluten free, they can be used as a direct substitute without having to alter any other part of a traditional recipe. Recommended brands: Bob's Red Mill Gluten Free 1-to-1 Baking Flour , cup4cup Gluten Free Multipurpose Flour , or you can make your own.

3. Avocado Oil

Avocado Oil works as an alternative to butter. It is also great as a substitute for coconut oil for moisture if you are allergic to coconuts or do not like its strong taste. Like coconut oil, it is also good for cooking at high temperatures. Brand pictured: Chosen Foods 100% Pure Avocado Oil .
